This site is home to our Spring 2020 Data Science and Analytics Capping Project. We have created a model that generates new inspirational quotes by just the click of a button! The quote generator can be found by clicking the Quote Generator Tab above.

Why Quotes ?
For our project we decided to generate inspirational quotes. The quotes we produce are short and sweet, while also conveying a positive message. We are posting our products on twitter, with the hope that reading a positive quote can also positively influence a persons day or attitude.

Our Data Set
The original dataset used to train the model is from Famous Quotes Database: https://thewebminer.com/buy-famous-quotes-database
