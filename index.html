Welcome to Quixotic Quotes!

This site is home to our Spring 2020 Data Science and Analytics Capping Project. We have created a model that generates new inspirational quotes by just the click of a button! The quote generator can be found by clicking the Quote Generator Tab above.

You can explore the process of how our project came to be, why we chose this topic and our original dataset by clicking on the About Project tab.

The Twitter Bot tab will redirect you to a webpage that shows what our twitter feed looks like. You can also follow out Quixotic Quotes Twitter Account here!

The Visualization page will show you some characteristics of our original data and compare some of it to the new additional data we have pulled from various twitter accounts. Here you will be able to see some of the statistical features of the dataset and draw some conclusions of your own.

You can learn more about the creators of this project and website by clicking on the About Us tab.
